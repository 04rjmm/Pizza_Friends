Welcome!

Pizza Friends was established in 1923 by the Pepperoni family and remains in the family to this day! Many things have changed through the years, from the variety of toppings available to the advance in wood pizza ovens, but one thing that remains is our determination to deliver great quality pizza.
We strive to make our restaurant feel like you are coming into our family home, so why not come in and see for yourself! Try our famous Four Cheese pizza, or maybe do it yourself and choose from a range of toppings including quorn for anyone looking to substitute meat!
We look forward to seeing you soon!
